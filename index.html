About me

Hi, I'm Divya! I'm currently pursuing my MCA from Graphic Era Deemed to be University, I have a strong interest in both coding and management. My technical expertise includes Python, Basics of C++, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design and develop user-friendly web applications.

In addition to coding, I find peace and creativity through Mandala art. This meditative art form not only helps me unwind but also sharpens my focus and problem-solving abilities, which directly benefits my approach to coding challenges. Alongside my passion for coding, I also have a deep interest in management, with expertise in Principles of Management and Business Communication.

Beyond academics, I love traveling, especially to mountainous regions, playing table tennis, and reading novels. My recent reads include Verity by Colleen Hoover and Karma by Sadhguru.
